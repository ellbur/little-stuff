NAME
       sl - display animations aimed to correct users who accidentally enter sl instead of ls.

SYNOPSIS
       sl [ -alFe ]

DESCRIPTION
       sl  Displays  animations  aimed  to  correct users who accidentally enter sl instead of ls.  SL
       stands for Steam Locomotive.

OPTIONS
       -a     An accident seems to happen. Youâ€™ll feel pity for people who cry for help.

       -l     shows little one.

       -F     It flies.

       -e     Allow interrupt by Ctrl+C.


SEE ALSO
       ls(1)

BUGS
       It rarely shows contents of current directory.
